The Itinerary

12 : 30 pm

Lunch at Bread Furst

Inside The Phillips Collection, 1600 21st St NW

A James Beard Award-winning bakery café right inside the museum — named one of the Washington Post's 5 best museum cafés in DC. The croissants are legendary (buttery, flaky, many layers), the pain au chocolat is a must, and their seasonal sandwiches use locally sourced ingredients that rotate on a whim. Pair with a Counter Culture coffee or a chai latte.

The Phillips Collection

1600 21st St NW, Washington, DC

America's first museum of modern art. Home to Renoir's Luncheon of the Boating Party and a beautiful rotating collection of impressionist and contemporary works. We wander at our own pace.
